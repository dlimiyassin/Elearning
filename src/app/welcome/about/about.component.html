About us

An online platform for learning

E-Learning is a mode of distance learning, in other words being able to follow your courses and have access to sometimes personalized online training from any place, via the Internet, from any computer tool. It consists to make modules containing courses available to a learner in order to facilitate access to resources 7 days a week, 24 hours a day, that is to say instead of traveling within a training center and having a teacher who gives the course once, the learner has the possibility of having a recording or file that can listen to it, watch it or read it until he understood. At the end of the diploma training, the students' results justify the degree of competence of the students, that is to say the students, who obtained their diplomas with average grades, have gaps in a few modules, which requires a solution of improvement. The proposed solution concerns the use of an E-Learning platform giving more lessons and more exercises to reinforce learning.
